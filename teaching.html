Teaching

My teaching experience has centered on undergraduate mathematics, especially in courses where conceptual clarity and careful problem solving are essential.

Teaching Areas

Undergraduate course

Discrete Mathematics

Support in logic, proof techniques, combinatorics, recurrence relations, and graph-based reasoning, with special attention to helping students gain confidence in formal arguments.

Undergraduate course

Mathematical Analysis

Teaching focused on limits, continuity, differentiation, integration, and rigorous reasoning about sequences and functions, balancing intuition with precision.

Teaching Approach

I value clear structure, patient explanation, and a gradual move from examples to full mathematical reasoning. The goal is not only to solve exercises, but to help students become more comfortable reading, writing, and discussing mathematics with confidence.
